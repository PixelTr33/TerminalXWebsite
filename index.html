Assignable Tasks for AI We're designing a system that allows you to make a large number of AI models to work together on a large scale to make you full scale applications with just a single prompt.
Mobile App for Development
Always free for the Indie Community Use the mobile app to assign tasks and develop from your phone on the go. Please keep in mind that your desktop must remain on for the mobile app to work though. We will always remain free for Indie developers who make less than $10,000AUD per year from their creations. You will be asked to provide your own API key though. Code is colored and formatted in Project TerminalX, this is just a web-demonstration.
We listen to our community We will always accept advice from all sorts of development backgrounds to help shape and make TerminalX better for the overall community that is using it to make software/apps.

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: When do you plan to release? A: Late 2025 to Early 2026.
Q: Will there be watermarks? A: No, not even on the free plan
Q: How much will TerminalX Cost A: Refer to the pricing plans above
Q: Where can I see updates? A: Either on the site or our Instagram account which is under the tag @project_terminalx.
Q: How do you afford the AI? A: We don't as users are to bring their own API key to access the AI services.
Q: What models are included? A: We hope to roll out various models from multiple cloud providers some of these include but are not limited to; GPT-5, GPT-4o, GPT-4, Claude-Sonnet-4 and Claude-Opus-4. We'll most likely add local models such as Qwen-Coder-4B in a future update.
Q: Will I need an account? A: Yes, to access TerminalX you will need an account with TerminalX. This is so we can verify that you own the product even on free accounts as free accounts must be verified to avoid abuse. What will you create with the power of AI?
